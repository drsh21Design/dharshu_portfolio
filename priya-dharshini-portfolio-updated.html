and this is my story...

A product designer who loves thoughtful, useful interfaces.

I first got interested in design by noticing how apps and websites looked, felt, and guided people from one step to the next. That curiosity slowly turned into a craft: understanding what makes a product feel simple, polished, and easy to use.

At 73 Strings, I design AI-powered fintech experiences for complex private-market data, dashboards, and decision workflows. I work across UX, UI, prototyping, design systems, and cross-functional collaboration.

I care about crisp labels, honest empty states, scalable components, and the small details that help people trust what they are seeing.

"Clarity is usually hiding inside the chaos. I just like finding it."
